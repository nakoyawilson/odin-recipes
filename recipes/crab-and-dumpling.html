Curried Crab (served with Dumplings)

Description

The official national dish of Tobago. Curried crab and dumplings is a flavorful crab dish cooked down in a curry sauce with coconut milk.

Curried Crab - Ingredients

Serves 8

  • 4 large crabs
  • 2 tbsp. margarine
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 tsp. minced garlic
  • 1 tnsp. minced green seasoning
  • ½ tsp. fresh or ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp. curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp. salt or to taste
  • hot pepper to taste
  • ½ cup water or more

Curried Crab - Steps

  1. Clean crab and cut into sections.
  2. Heat margarine and sauté onion, garlic, green seasoning and ginger.
  3. Mix curry with coconut milk and add to onion and garlic; cook for a minute.
  4. Add crab, salt, pepper and water; bring to the boil and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Cover tightly, lower heat and cook for 25-30 minutes; add more water if needed. Adjust salt and pepper.
  6. Serve with dumplings (A very popular Tobagonian dish).

Dumplings - Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ½ cup milk or water

Steps

  1. Combine flour,baking powder and salt.
  2. Stir in milk or water, dough should be soft enough to be dropped from spoon.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of dough on hot stew mixture.
  4. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.

Source: The Multi-Cultural Cuisine of Trinidad & Tobago and the Caribbean
